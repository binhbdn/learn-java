BJP5 Self-Check 8.22: abstraction

What is abstraction?
The ability to focus on a problem at a high level without worrying about the minor details.

How do objects provide abstraction?
By giving us more powerful pieces of data that have sophisticated behavior without having to manage and manipulate the data directly.
