BJP5 Self-Check 7.2: numbersElements

Assume that the following array has been defined, though you do not necessarily know its length.

int[] numbers = new int[??];
What expression should be used:

To access the first element?	numbers[0]
To access the last element, assuming was created with length 10?	numbers[9]
To access its last element, regardless of its length?	numbers[numbers.length-1]
