BJP5 Self-Check 2.17: maxMin
What is the output from the following code?

int max;
int min = 10;
max = 17 - 4 / 10;
max = max + 6;
min = max - min;
System.out.println(max * 2);
System.out.println(max + min);
System.out.println(max);
System.out.println(min);

output
46
36
23
13
