BJP5 Self-Check 2.6: declareRealNumberSyntax
Which of the following choices is the correct syntax for declaring a real number variable named 'grade' and initializing its value to 4.0?

a       grade = double 4.0;
b       grade = 4;
c       int grade : 4.0;
d       4.0 = grade;
e   x   double grade = 4.0;
